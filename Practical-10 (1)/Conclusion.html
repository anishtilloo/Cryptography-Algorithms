Conclusion

It is a special type of cryptography in which polyalphabetic substitution cipher is used which is like monoalphabetic substitution cipher except for one change. The alphabet that is taken as a cipher changes periodically while encoding the plaintext. This reduces the cipher less susceptible to attacks using cryptanalysis of letter frequencies.

Cryptanalysis :-

1. The Vigenere cipher was though to be completely unbreakable for hundreds of years, and indeed, if very long keys are used the vigenere cipher can be unbreakable. But if short keys are used, or if we have a lot of ciphertext compared to the key length, the vigenere cipher is quite solvable.

2. Identify the period of the cipher (the length of the key), then find the specific key.
